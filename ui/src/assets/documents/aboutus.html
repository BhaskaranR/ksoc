TradeMark

KarmaSoc offers the following services, but not limited to: Internet-based social networking services; Internet-based social introduction and social networking services; On-line social networking services; Online social networking services provided through a community website; Online social networking services accessible by means of downloadable mobile applications; Providing a social networking website for entertainment purposes; Providing on-line computer databases and on-line searchable databases in the field of social networking

Business Description

KarmaSoc is a social network platform that incentivizes EVERY user for their constructive social activity, rewards them for registering local businesses to avail the offered deals, and offers them the ability to open ecommerce shop to trade within or their extended network.

Company Background – Problem / Solution

Social networks have several pain areas, which KarmaSoc aims to address: · Firms earn millions at the expense of users · Only active and professional social users make money and not all · Users fell they are wasting time checking social media · Rewarding and incentivizing mechanisms and can be abused · Products and Service providers have trouble finding buyers Our goal is to be a leading global social networking platform where every user will be a true partner and earn rewards by socializing constructively. For those who do not want to earn rewards, they can donate to a charity of their choice.

Products/Services

KarmaSoc is a social networking and ecommerce platform currently built as a Progressive Web App (PWA). Users can post various types of social content and segregate them for clarity. The ecommerce platform allows users to open shops for free and promote their products and services within their network eliminating the need for external marketing channels to acquire customers. Local deals are shown on a map to cater the needs of users seeking savings.

Technologies/Special Know-how

KarmaSoc has a unique proprietary pricing algorithm that drives the rewards and incentives mechanism, which is critical to our business model and robust systematic growth. The PWA comes with a powerful technology stack with a scalable elastic architecture. We use Nodejs with microservices containerized with docker. Devops is automated and is seamlessly deployed into AWS using Kubernetes. We use mongodb and MySQL as our databases.

Market

Target market includes the saturated yet evolving US social media segment where 68% of total population is registered. The market also includes all small businesses that want to promote deals locally and represent their brand as community supporters. We also cover aspiring entrepreneurs who want to start an online business and offer products and services. We aim niche demographics ages 13-34 (96 million), housewives, and all those who want to reap benefits for their time spent on social networks. This demographic has the highest chances of converting as people can use the money earned to purchase games or other necessary products. We also target those who do not want rewards but can donate to charities of their liking making a positive social impact.

Distribution Channels

Our sources of revenue are multi pronged. User’s active social involvement generates advertisement income. Users can open ecommerce shops and pay commissions on sales and pay for promoting their brands. Businesses register and pay consistent membership fees to promote their deals locally. KarmaSoc will reach the targeted market through comprehensive marketing strategies, including advertisements, digital marketing, blogs, word of mouth referrals, and innovative techniques such as appointing representatives in each educational institute and volunteers in each region. Users will act sales agents to register local businesses.

Competition

Among competitors are traditional social platforms such as Facebook, Google+, YouTube, Groupon, Yext, Nextdoor, FourSquare, among others, which do not incentivize users and allow only active and professional users to benefit monetarily. We also compete with evolving blockchain technology firms including, Steemit, Synereo, Nexus, and Yours, which incentivize users for their content. KarmaSoc is distinct from any of these firms as we do not fully rely on an incentivizing business model but are developing a sustainable growth model offering innovative ways for users to be actively involved, thereby mutually benefiting each other.

Current State

Incubated and developed a world-class PWA. Social functionalities, local deals on a map, and ecommerce are functional. Various useful sections are created for ease of navigation and to eliminate messy content segregation. Local businesses can promote deals on a map as per user’s geo-location. Aspiring entrepreneurs can open ecommerce shops.

Future State

KarmaSoc will be a global app and will penetrate each market strategically. We will be one-stop shop for all types of incentives. Funding will help achieve these goals and also help with hiring, creating a native app, enhancing the current version of PWA, getting direct access to deals from companies, and offering cash back programs. Lastly, we are working on a Proof of Concept to use blockchain technology to implement micropayments. We intend to issue an ICO (Initial Coin Offering) to raise capital and be in the forefront of blockchain technology.
